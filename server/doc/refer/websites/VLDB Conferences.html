The VLDB Conferences constitute one of the most eminent venues for the timely dissemination of research and development results in the field of database management. This series is perhaps the most international (in terms of participation, technical content, organization, and location) among all comparable events.

VLDB proceedings are distributed by Morgan Kaufmann Publishers. The Proceedings of the first 23 conferences (1975-1998) are included (as soft copy) in the ACM SIGMOD Anthology. The Endowment is committed to the inclusion of the future Proceedings (1998-...) in the ACM DiSC series.

The full text of all VLDB Conference papers from 1982-2004 is available online from http://www.vldb.org/ These can be reached by clicking the appropriate conference and then the appropriate "Electronic Edition" link of the paper.
