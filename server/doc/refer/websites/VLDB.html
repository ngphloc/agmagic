Very Large Data Base Endowment Inc. (VLDB Endowment) is non-profit organisation incorporated in the United States for the sole purpose of promoting and exchanging scholarly work in databases and related fields throughout the world.

 One of its vehicles for achieving its objectives is the sponsorship and support of the annual VLDB Conferences.
 Since 1992, the Endowment has started to publish a quarterly journal, The VLDB Journal for disseminating archival research results. The VLDB Journal is published in collaboration with Springer-Verlag.

  The VLDB historical archives (maintained by Shojiro Nishio) since 1995 contains web sites of past conferences, conference statistics (e.g., number of papers submitted, acceptance rates), and other useful information.

  The full text VLDB Conf. 2004 is available from this server.

 The Endowment also organizes a tutorial series to disseminate basic and advanced knowledge related to the field of database management to the developing nations worldwide.

 The library program provides libraries in developing countries, who ordinarily would not be able to afford to subscribe to the VLDB Conference Proceedings and The VLDB Journal, with this valuable material in order to support database research.

 The VLDB Endowment is governed by a Board of Trustees consisting of 21 members.
